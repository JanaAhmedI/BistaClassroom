What's this about?

This Application is a web-based platform designed to support preparation for SKD CPNS Polstat STIS tryouts. It allows users to register as either students or teachers. Teachers can create and manage tryouts and quizzes, while students can take part in available tryouts. This platform is managed by the Bina Statistik Team of BPS Provinsi Maluku Utara. {% endblock %}
